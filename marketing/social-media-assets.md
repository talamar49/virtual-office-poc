### Post 5: "Behind the Build" — Technical (לקהל מפתחים)
```
Building an isometric engine from scratch in React Canvas.

Here's what nobody tells you:

↳ Isometric math is actually simple (it's just 2D with a transform)
↳ Depth sorting is the hard part — painters algorithm with dynamic objects
↳ Pixel art requires `imageSmoothingEnabled = false` everywhere
↳ Sprite animations: slice from spritesheet each frame

[Code snippet or screenshot]

The hardest problem? Making it feel ALIVE.
→ Lerp movement (agents glide, not teleport)
→ Breathing animation (subtle sin wave bob)
→ Working indicator (animated dots above busy agents)

Full breakdown coming soon. 

Or just try Virtual Office and see it yourself: [link]

#WebDev #Canvas #React #PixelArt #AI #BuildInPublic
```
